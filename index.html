Mental Health Is Important

Welcome to my Blog, my name is Miguel Taveras and I am writing about the Mental Health Crisis in the United States of America. The pandemic of 2020 changed the world forever. Mental health is on the Rise in the United States. Men women and children of all ages have been affected in one way or another. If you know someone who is going through a hard time in life, offer your support, whether it's getting them professional help or being there for them in these tough times.

According to an article by CNN about 1 in 5 adults received mental health services in the past year.


Above are some videos, resources, and links to websites that may help you or a loved one if your are going through mental health problems and want to speak to a professional and seek help. There is nothing wrong with seeking help during these hard times. The pandemic of 2020 was once in a lifetime pandemic that changed every ones lives and with that being said, my name is Miguel Taveras, thank you for reading my website.
